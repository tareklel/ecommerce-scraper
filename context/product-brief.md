# Product Brief

[Name TBD] is an Arabic-first luxury fashion discovery platform for Saudi Arabia and later the GCC. It brings together stock, in local currency, and availability from luxury online retailers that ship to Saudi first — giving aspiring Saudi buyers, women first, a single destination to browse everything they can actually order to their country. The brands are the names they love: Dolce & Gabbana, Valentino, Gucci, and their peers. The value proposition is range and convenience: the widest selection available in Saudi, all in one place, with sale and discount information surfaced through filters and design rather than front-and-centre messaging — the identity is discovery and range, not cheapest price. The experience is clean, minimal, and mobile-first, closer to a well-edited luxury retailer than a comparison engine, with Arabic as the primary language and new arrivals as the default view. Early releases focus on product listing and detail pages; cross-site product matching, price history, and price alerts are mid-term features.
